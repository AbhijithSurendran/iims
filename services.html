    Our Services

    QHSE Services

    • QHSE Trainings
    • QHSE General Trainings
    • QHSE Certified Trainings

    Consultancy

    • Quality Management System ISO 9001:2015, Environmental management System ISO 14001:2015, Occupational Health & safety Management System ISO 45001:2018.

    Other Services

    • QHSEMS Internal Auditing, Environmental monitoring services, HSE Risk Assessment, HSEMS ERP Development.
    • Postgraduate training for Doctors and healthcare providers; basic and advance level of courses for healthcare providers, emergency medical technicians and first responders.
    • Emergency Medical Care, First Aid and Trauma Care training for employees of Institutions.
    • Basic and Advanced Disaster Management training programs.
    • American Heart Association (AHA) and International Trauma Life Support (ITLS) certified training programs for healthcare providers.
    • Advanced Trauma Care for Doctors and Nurses.
    • Training for NGOs, Community Based Organizations (CBO) and Civil Societies in various emergency medical response programs.
    • Basic and Advanced Disaster Management training programs.

    Consulting Services For Setting Up

    • Emergency Department (ED) development and training in establishing Emergency & Disaster Standard Operating Protocols (E&D-SOP) for Hospitals.
    • Emergency Medical Services (EMS).
    • Training for EMS crew, EMTs, and Firs Responders.
    • Industrial/Occupational Emergency Medical Training for employees.
    • Specialized training for first responders such as Paramedics, Police, Fire and Para-Military forces in Mass Casualty Management, Triage, Extrication, Rescue, First Aid & Trauma Care, Casualty Immobilization and Transportation etc.
    • Training of Instructors in emergency medical care and safety training programs.
    • Disaster Management Training for National/State/Local Government organizations and allied response personnel.
