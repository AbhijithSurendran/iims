    Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS)

    ACLS is an advanced, instructor-led classroom course that highlights the importance of team dynamics and communication, systems of care and immediate post-cardiac-arrest care. It also covers airway management and related pharmacology. In this course, skills are taught in large, group sessions and small, group learning and testing stations where case-based scenarios are presented.

    Indian Institute of Emergency Medical Services

    IIEMS is a premier Emergency Medicine, Trauma Care, Emergency Medical Services and Disaster Management training institute in India having its headquarters in Kerala-India also popularly known as God’s Own Country. Our international operation is managed by the Executive Office in Troy, Pennsylvania & USA.

    Key Features of IIEMS

    International Training

    Initiated the First International Training Program in Emergency medicine in India.

    Training Programs

    Conducted over 7000 courses and training programs across India at various locations.

    Training Sites

    Set up several Training sites and regional training centers across India providing courses and training programs in Emergency Medicine.

    Training Expertise

    We have more than 300 instructors in our network across India for conducting various courses globally. We are the only training institution in India holding more than 8 international affiliations for conduct of EMS, Trauma and Disaster Management courses.

    Call 102 (EMS System)

    Developed and successfully running an EMS System (Call 102) aimed to provide immediate, on-scene and en-route emergency medical care to accident and other emergency victims across the state regardless of their ability to pay.

    Accomplishments

    No.1 AHA Training Centre in India and No.2 in Asia Conducted 7,500 training programs and trained more than 3,62,000 personnel till date.
